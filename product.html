DBM stands for Disel Bitumen Macadam, which is a type of road surfacing material that is made by laying layers of crushed stone, knonw as aggregate, and then binding them together with bitumen. The resulting surface is highly durable and resistant to wear and tear.

DBM

Precoated chippings, also known as pre-coated aggregates, are small stones or gravel that are coated with a layer of bitumen or other material. These chippings are commonly used in road construction to provide a durable, skid-resistant surface.

Precoated chippings

+

K1-60 is not a term commonly used in the context of road construction or materials. Without more context, it is difficult to determine what this term might refer to.

K1-60

Asphalt concrete, also commonly referred to as asphalt or hot mix asphalt (HMA), is a durable and versatile material used extensively in road construction and paving. It is a mixture of aggregates, such as crushed stone, gravel, or sand, and asphalt binder, which is a petroleum-based glue-like substance.

Asphalt Concrete

+

IDO is a type of fuel that is commonly used in industrial applications, including in generators and heavy machinery. It is a type of diesel fuel that has been refined to meet specific industrial requirements, such as high efficiency and low emissions.

Industrial Diesel Oil

MC30 and MC3000 are both types of bitumen emulsions, which are mixtures of bitumen and water. They are used as a binding agent for asphalt and other road construction materials.
