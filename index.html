We Provide Services

Hand Roller refer to a heavy duty machine that is used to compact and smooth out surfaces such as roads, parking lots, and other construction sites. The purpose of roller services is to improve the stability, strength, and durability of the surface, as well as to ensure proper drainage and levelness.

Hand Roller

+

Precoated chippings, also known as pre-coated aggregates, are small stones or gravel that are coated with a layer of bitumen or other material. These chippings are commonly used in road construction to provide a durable, skid-resistant surface.

Precoated Chippings

+

Is a piece of heavy equipment used for laying asphalt or concrete on roads, parking lots, and other flat surfaces. These machines come in different sizes and designs, but they all share the same basic function of evenly distributing and compacting asphalt or concrete over a surface.

Paver Machine

+

Asphalt concrete, also known as asphalt pavement, is a composite material made of asphalt binder and aggregates (such as sand, gravel, or crushed stone) that is commonly used for paving roads, parking lots, and other surfaces.

Asphalt Concrete

+

A bitumen distributor is a specialized vehicle designed to distribute hot liquid bitumen or asphalt to various construction sites. A bitumen distributor typically consists of a tank that is used to store and heat the bitumen to a specific temperature, a heating system, a pump, a spray bar, and a control system.

Bitumen Distributor

+

Is facility that manufactures asphalt concrete, also known as hot mix asphalt (HMA), which is a mixture of aggregate (such as gravel, sand, or stone) and liquid asphalt cement. The asphalt production plant is responsible for heating and mixing the aggregate and asphalt cement to produce the final product.
